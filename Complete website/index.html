Welcome to the RCAI Lab's Autonomous Vehicle Project!

RCAI Lab's Autonomous Vehicle Project, a pioneering educational and research initiative in Pakistan. We are committed to advancing knowledge and expertise in autonomous vehicles, exploring innovative approaches in ICT and cost-effective product design. Through our cutting-edge research, we aim to shape the future of transportation in Pakistan and beyond.

Meet Our Team

Dr. Muhammad Khurram

An Associate Professor at Department of Computer & Information Systems Engineering - NED UET. He is also working as a Principal Investigator at Smart City Lab - NCAI and heading the complete team of Co-PIs, Team Lead, Research Associates and Assistants. He completed his Bachelors and Masters in Computer Systems from NED University and went to New Zealand at Massey University for PHD in IC Design. Khurram is also Director at Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (RCAI) at CIS Department.
